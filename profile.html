I married a great guy I met while working in the Digital Media Division at Microsoft. He worked in engineering and "crossed over to the dark-side" to marry someone in marketing. We now have two great girls, Riley and Reina and recently adopted a golden lab named Max and live on Capitol Hill in Seattle.
